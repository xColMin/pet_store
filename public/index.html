Very Important Pets is an all-volunteer, non-profit, animal welfare organization dedicated to the rescue and placement of stray, neglected and abused animals. All VIPets is completely funded through fundraising efforts and public donations.

All of our pets are cared for by foster homes and receive complete medical care, which includes worming, vaccinations, spaying/neutering and microchipping prior to their adoption. The fees included in this site represent the cost of the process of adoption.
